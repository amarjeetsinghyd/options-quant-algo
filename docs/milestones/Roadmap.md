# Engineering Roadmap

This roadmap defines the sequential progression of the Scientific Research Institution.
Milestones are only undertaken once the previous phase has achieved total stability.

*   **Phase 1 - 6:** Foundational Engineering & Architecture (COMPLETED)
*   **Phase 7:** Stabilization (Data Collection & Observation Tuning)
*   **Phase 8:** Replay Engine
*   **Phase 9:** Feature Laboratory
*   **Phase 10:** Dataset Health Monitor
*   **Phase 11:** Experiment Framework
*   **Phase 12:** Representation Learning
*   **Phase 13:** Transformer Research
*   **Phase 14:** Autonomous Scientific Observer
